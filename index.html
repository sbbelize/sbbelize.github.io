Who We Are

Southern Baptist of Belize is the union of three organizations with a common mission; to equip the Church to make disciples.

We each adhere to The Baptist Faith and Message 2000 as our confession of faith.

The vision behind Southern Baptist of Belize is for a network of churches to grow and extend the Kingdom of God across Belize and the world.
